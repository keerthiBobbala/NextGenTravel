Tripura-Agartala

Overview

Tripura blends royal heritage, tribal culture, and natural beauty into a serene and offbeat travel experience.

Attractions

Ujjayanta Palace

A majestic royal residence turned museum showcasing Tripura’s rich cultural heritage.

Neermahal

A stunning lake palace blending Hindu and Mughal architecture, set in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake.

Activities

  • Ujjayanta Palace: Explore the royal architecture and beautiful gardens of the Ujjayanta Palace in Agartala.

  • Jampui Hills Trekking:Enjoy scenic views and vibrant orange orchards while trekking in Jampui Hills.

  • Neermahal Palace: Visit the stunning water palace located in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake.

Accommodation

Here are some recommended places to stay:

  • Ujjayanta Palace - Stay at nearby hotels like the Hotel Sonar Tori for a comfortable visit to the palace.

  • Ginger Hotel Agartala -A modern hotel offering comfortable rooms and amenities, located near Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport.

  • Hotel Polo Towers Agartala - A luxury hotel featuring upscale facilities, including a pool and restaurant, situated in the heart of the city.

  • Hotel Rajdhani - A budget-friendly option with spacious rooms and a central location, providing good value for money. ​

  • Eden Tourist Lodge - A government-run lodge offering eco-friendly accommodations with modern amenities, set amidst scenic landscapes.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit - October to March (pleasant weather for sightseeing)

  • Respect the Environment - Dispose of waste properly and avoid littering in natural areas.

  • Safety - Keep your belongings secure and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded places.

Transportation

  • Auto-rickshaws - Common for short-distance travel within cities, easily available for quick trips.

  • Shared Taxis - Affordable and convenient for group travel, commonly used for inter-city travel.

  • Private Cabs - A comfortable option for personalized travel within and outside the city, bookable through apps or local agencies.

  • Buses - Government-run buses connect major towns and cities, ideal for budget travelers.
