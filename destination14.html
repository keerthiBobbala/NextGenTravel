Haryana - Kurukshetra

Overview

Haryana, in northern India, blends rich history and modernity, with landmarks like Kurukshetra's Mahabharata ties and Gurgaon's IT prominence. It's also a vital agricultural and industrial hub.

Attractions

Kurukshetra

Known as the "Land of the Bhagavad Gita," this historic city is tied to the Mahabharata and features sites like Brahma Sarovar and Jyotisar.

Pinjore Gardens

Pinjore Gardens, a stunning Mughal-style garden in Haryana, offers serene landscapes and historic charm, perfect for a relaxing getaway.

Activities

  • Stroll Through the Terraces:Explore the beautifully landscaped seven terraces with fountains, pavilions, and lush greenery.

  • Picnicking:Relax and enjoy a picnic amidst the serene surroundings.

  • Photography:Capture the stunning Mughal-style architecture and vibrant flora.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit - Plan your trip between October and March when the weather is pleasant.

  • Explore Nearby Attractions - Don't miss nearby landmarks like Chandigarh and Shimla, which are easily accessible.

  • Cash and ATMs - Carry some cash as not all places accept cards, and ATMs can be sparse in remote areas.

  • Respect the Environment - Dispose of waste properly and avoid littering in natural areas.

  • Safety - Keep your belongings secure and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded places.

Transportation

  • Haryana Roadways - The state's public bus service provides economical and reliable travel across cities and towns.

  • Railways -Haryana is well-connected by Indian Railways, with major stations like Ambala, Gurgaon, and Kurukshetra.

  • Air Travel - The nearest airport is Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi, serving Haryana and neighboring regions.

  • Private Transport - Taxis, auto-rickshaws, and app-based cab services like Ola and Uber are widely available.
