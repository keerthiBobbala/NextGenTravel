Assam - Kaziranga National Park

Overview

Kaziranga National Park in Assam is a UNESCO World Heritage site, famed for its thriving population of the great Indian one-horned rhinoceros. Visitors can enjoy thrilling jeep and elephant safaris to explore the park's diverse flora and fauna.

Attractions

Kaziranga National Park

Famous for its population of the great Indian one-horned rhinoceros, it offers thrilling jeep and elephant safaris to explore its diverse flora and fauna.

Kakochang Waterfalls

Located near the park, these picturesque waterfalls are perfect for a refreshing break amidst nature.

Activities

  • Jeep Safari:Embark on an exciting jeep safari to explore the park's diverse wildlife, including the great Indian one-horned rhinoceros.

  • Elephant Safari: Experience the thrill of an elephant safari, offering a unique perspective on the park's flora and fauna.

  • Bird Watching:Discover a variety of bird species in their natural habitat, making it a paradise for bird enthusiasts.

Accommodation

Here are some recommended places to stay:

  • Resort Borgos: -A luxurious resort offering comfortable rooms, a spa, and a swimming pool.

  • IORA - The Retreat - A 4-star resort with spacious rooms, a swimming pool, and a variety of dining options.

  • Infinity Resorts Kaziranga - A serene resort with well-appointed rooms, a swimming pool, and beautiful surroundings.

  • Dhanshree Resort - A mid-range resort with cozy rooms, a restaurant, and easy access to the park.

  • Kaziranga Florican Lodge - A budget-friendly lodge with clean rooms and good service.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit - The best time to visit Kaziranga National Park is from November to April.

  • Stay Hydrated - The desert climate can be dehydrating, so drink plenty of water and carry a water bottle.

  • Cash and ATMs - Carry some cash as not all places accept cards, and ATMs can be sparse in remote areas.

  • Respect the Environment - Dispose of waste properly and avoid littering in natural areas.

  • Safety - Keep your belongings secure and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded places.

Transportation

  • By Air - Fly to Jorhat or Guwahati Airport and hire a taxi or bus to Kaziranga.

  • By Train -Take a train to Furkating Junction and then a taxi or bus to the park.

  • By Road - Use regular bus services from nearby cities like Guwahati, Jorhat, and Tezpur.

  • Local Transport - Once at the park, use jeeps and elephant safaris to explore the area.
