Arunachal Pradesh - Tawang

Overview

Arunachal Pradesh's Tawang is a high-altitude town renowned for its stunning Tawang Monastery, breathtaking landscapes, and serene lakes. It offers a unique blend of spirituality and natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination in northeastern India.

Attractions

Tawang Monastery

Also known as the Golden Namgyal Lhatse, this is the second-largest monastery in the world and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Madhuri Lake

Also known as Sangetsar Lake, this picturesque lake is surrounded by the Himalayas and offers a tranquil escape.

Activities

  • Visit Tawang Monastery:Explore the second-largest monastery in the world and soak in the spiritual ambiance.

  • Trek to Sela Pass: Experience breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains and the serene alpine lake.

  • Relax at Madhuri Lake:Enjoy the tranquility and picturesque surroundings of this beautiful lake.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit - The best time to visit Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh, is from March to June and September to October.

  • Stay Hydrated - The desert climate can be dehydrating, so drink plenty of water and carry a water bottle.

  • Cash and ATMs - Carry some cash as not all places accept cards, and ATMs can be sparse in remote areas.

  • Respect the Environment - Dispose of waste properly and avoid littering in natural areas.

  • Safety - Keep your belongings secure and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded places.

Transportation

  • Public Buses - The most affordable option, available from the bus stand

  • Shared Sumos -A popular choice for traveling between towns and tourist spots.

  • Private Taxis - Convenient for personalized travel and exploring remote areas.

  • Two-Wheeler Rentals - Ideal for those who prefer exploring at their own pace.
