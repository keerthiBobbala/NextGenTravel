Meghalaya

Overview

Meghalaya, the "Abode of Clouds," is renowned for its lush landscapes, vibrant culture, and abundant rainfall. It offers natural wonders like living root bridges and Nohkalikai Falls, making it a paradise for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Attractions

Double Decker Living Root Bridge

The Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Cherrapunjee is a remarkable bridge formed from the living roots of trees. This unique structure is a testament to the ingenuity of local Khasi tribes and offers a fascinating trekking experience amidst lush greenery.

Shillong

Shillong, the capital of Meghalaya, boasts picturesque landscapes and a vibrant cultural scene. Its pleasant climate and charming attractions make it a popular destination for travelers.

Activities

  • Boating at Umiam LakeEnjoy the serene beauty of this picturesque man-made lake.

  • Exploring Ward's Lake A charming lake in the heart of Shillong, perfect for a leisurely stroll or paddle boating.

  • Visiting Shillong PeakGet a panoramic view of the entire city and its surroundings from the highest point.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit - : Visit between October and May for the best weather and scenic beauty.

  • Stay Hydrated - The desert climate can be dehydrating, so drink plenty of water and carry a water bottle.

  • Cash and ATMs - Carry some cash as not all places accept cards, and ATMs can be sparse in remote areas.

  • Respect the Environment - Dispose of waste properly and avoid littering in natural areas.

  • Safety - Keep your belongings secure and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded places.

Transportation

  • Buses - Local buses are available for public transportation within Leh and nearby areas. They are a budget-friendly option but may not be as comfortable as taxis.

  • Taxis -Taxis are a convenient way to travel within Ladakh. You can book taxis through local taxi services like Ladakh Taxi Booking or Tripvito.

  • Rented Vehicles - You can rent cars or bikes to explore Ladakh at your own pace. Popular options include SUVs like Toyota Innova, Scorpio, and Tata Sumo.

  • Bike Rentals - For the adventurous, bike rentals are available for exploring the rugged terrain and scenic routes of Ladakh.
